Baba Baidyanath Dham Temple

Deoghar is home to the Baidyanath Jyotirlinga Temple, also known as Baba Baidyanath Dham. It is one of the twelve Jyotirlingas, the most sacred abodes of Shiva. This temple is a significant pilgrimage site for Hindus.
